Follow Rugby has been designed to make it easier for anyone interested in rugby to follow its key developments on Twitter.

The data has been organised into relevant widget groupings so you can see the latest real time updates - simply interact as you would on Twitter: follow, mention, reply, retweet, favorite etc!

Our Match Centre provides head to head widgets of both teams, which allows you to see the perspective of both sides during the match. A widget for the match #hashtag is also shown – join in the match conversation. The match centre will be updated before each round of major matches and is a great way to follow live score and key action updates on the internet.*

To help the site develop please follow @@FollowRugbySite and suggest new Rugby Twitter accounts for us to add to the site. For longer comments and enquiries, please send an e-mail to ap@@follow-rugby.com.






*We cannot guarantee that all teams will publish updates for all matches but the official accounts of club and international sides are increasingly Tweeting during games – it is a great way to follow the action.
