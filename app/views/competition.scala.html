Competition Rules

To enter:

  • Select the players that you believe will be announced by Warren Gatland at the official tour party launch in London on April 30th.
  • When you are 100% happy with your squad, enter your full name, a password, contact number and e-mail address - finally click "Enter Competition".
  • The squad is likely to consist of 37 names so we have capped the competition at this total. You will only be able to submit your squad by clicking "Enter Competition" if you have selected 37 players.
  • Up to the competition deadline you can change your entry by clicking "Load Saved Squad" and entering your e-mail address and password into the login box.
  • Maximum of one entry per person and e-mail address.
  • There are no limits on how many players can be selected from each position, as we will not know the squad configuration until Warren Gatland announces the final touring party (and it does not matter what position a player appears in as entrants are simply guessing if the player is going to be on the plane to Australia, via Hong Kong).
  • All entries must be in by April 25th 10pm BST, so 5 days before the squad announcement. This is to avoid Lions press leak competition sabotage!

Scoring:

  • 1 point for each correct player.
  • The two entrants with the most points will each win an official Adidas British and Irish 2013 Lions shirt. In the event of a tie on the winning score(s) there will be a draw to decide who wins the official prizes.
