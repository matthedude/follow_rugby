To celebrate the launch of Follow-Rugby.com we have created a competition to end the greatest rugby clubhouse and pub debates of the year. Which players will be chosen to tour with the 2013 British and Irish Lions? And who will Warren Gatland choose to captain the side?

We have compiled an extensive list of possible tourists for you to choose from. If you want to include a player not listed please contact @@FollowRugbySite and we will add the player as an option.

To enter:

  • Select the players and captain that you believe will be announced by Warren Gatland at the official tour party launch in London on April 30th.
  • When you are 100% happy with your squad, enter your full name, a password, contact number and e-mail address - finally click SUBMIT. Up to the competition deadline you can then change your team by entering your email and password into the login box.
  • Maximum of one entry per person and e-mail address.
  • There are no limits on how many players can be selected from each position, as we will not know the squad configuration until Warren Gatland announces his squad (and it does not matter what position a player appears in as entrants are simply guessing if the player is going to make the squad).
  • All entries must be in by April 25th 12pm BST. This is to avoid Lions press leak competition sabotage!

Scoring:

  • 1 point for each correct player.
  • 2 bonus points for guessing the official Lions captain. Leave the field blank if you believe that Gatland will not announce a test captain at the official Lions squad announcement launch - if this is correct you will also score 2 points.
  • The winners will be the entrants with the most points. If more than 3 entrants are tied there will be a draw to decide who wins the official prizes.

Prizes:

The three entrants with the highest points totals will each receive an Official Adidas British and Irish 2013 Lions shirt!

We hope you enjoy www.follow-rugby.com.
